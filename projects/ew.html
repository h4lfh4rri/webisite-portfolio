EASY WEDDING

2022 UI designer

Challenges
Easy Weddings needed a website redesign to meet the growing demands of their marketing efforts and integrate new, customer-centric features. The goal was to create a seamless, all-in-one experience for couples planning their dream DIY weddings while enhancing usability and engagement..

Solution
The project involved a comprehensive overhaul of the visual design, including layouts and content presentation. Collaborating closely with stakeholders, we explored and refined innovative ideas, translating them into practical design solutions. The redesign prioritised responsiveness, consistent user flows, and an intuitive interface for key modules, ensuring a cohesive and engaging experience across devices.

Outcomes
Following the successful launch of the redesigned UI, Easy Weddings experienced a notable increase in leads and conversions. The improved platform has made them more competitive in the industry, strengthening their position as a go-to resource for wedding planning.
