OTHER DESIGN HIGHLIGHTS

2020-2023 Various Roles

Showcasing a selection of my design works and case studies, this portfolio highlights projects ranging from e-commerce webpage design and mobile app interfaces to logo revamps and in-depth UX case studies. Each project reflects my commitment to creating user-focused and visually engaging designs tailored to meet specific goals and challenges.

These works represent my journey across various roles, from collaborating with teams on diverse projects to my time as a freelancer. These experiences have provided invaluable opportunities to put into practice the design skills I honed during my studies at art college. I’ve been able to apply foundational design theories, experiment with creative solutions, and sharpen my overall understanding of visual design principles. Throughout this journey, I’ve also benefited greatly from professional insights and guidance. Collaborating with talented designers has been an enriching experience, and I’m especially grateful to those who mentored me along the way. Their advice and support have significantly shaped my approach to design and contributed to my growth as a professional.
